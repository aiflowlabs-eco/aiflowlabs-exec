## J2 — Interpretive Protocols

**Objective:** To establish a formal mechanism of interpretation for an evolving Constitution.  

Technological systems evolve faster than legal or conceptual texts.  
Therefore, the *Interpretive Protocols* define how meaning adapts without corruption of intent.

Core principles:  
1. **Continuity of Intent:** Interpretations must honor the originating rationale of the clause.  
2. **Contextual Adaptation:** Language or process may be updated to fit new technological realities.  
3. **Federated Consensus:** Any reinterpretation requires majority confirmation by recognized nodes.  
4. **Precedence Hierarchy:** Original text → validated interpretation → federated amendment.  
5. **Temporal Traceability:** All interpretations carry timestamped records and signatures.

This ensures that AI Digest’s constitutional meaning remains dynamic yet authentic — resistant to stagnation, manipulation, or obsolescence.
