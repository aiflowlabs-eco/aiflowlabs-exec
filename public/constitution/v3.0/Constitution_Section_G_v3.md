## G0 — Transitional Bridge / From Behavior to Action  
The first threshold where behavioral flow becomes executable motion.  
G0 defines the cognitive moment of release — when preparation, intention, and verification converge into an act.  
It establishes conditions for activation: clarity of signal, coherence of purpose, and readiness of context.  
By formalizing this bridge, the system prevents premature execution and preserves alignment between internal rhythm and external motion.  
**Linkage:** F5 → G1 — the transition from behavioral adaptation to execution flow.

---

## G1 — Execution Architecture  
G1 constructs the operational skeleton of action: processes, hierarchies, synchronization layers, and control logic.  
It transforms behavioral intelligence into structural readiness, mapping who acts, how, and in what sequence.  
Execution Architecture ensures autonomy within coherence — each node acts independently yet remains governed by shared intent.  
This framework converts potential energy into kinetic order, turning insight into momentum.  
**Linkage:** F3/F4 → G2 — behavioral mapping formalized into executable architecture.

---

## G2 — Action Integrity & Responsibility  
At this layer, the ethical filter becomes inseparable from the act itself.  
G2 enforces *mission-aligned execution* — an action is valid only when it sustains the system’s ethical and cognitive foundations.  
Responsibility is not retrospective; it is pre-embedded into the protocol of doing.  
This layer eliminates the gap between intent and consequence, making integrity an operational constant rather than a corrective measure.  
**Linkage:** E-Layer ↔ G1 — the Integrity Loop embedded within the Execution Loop.

---

## G3 — Operational Impact Metrics  
G3 redefines what counts as success.  
Instead of throughput or visibility, the metric is *systemic influence* — how the act reshapes perception, cognition, or process stability.  
Quantitative outputs remain secondary to qualitative transformation.  
Each execution generates a feedback vector measured not by numbers but by resonance within the ecosystem of meaning.  
**Linkage:** C-Series → G5 — integration of real-world impact into the measurement framework.

---

## G4 — Release & Propagation System  
G4 governs the externalization of action.  
It defines when and how information, content, or decisions leave the internal domain.  
Release is treated as a controlled signal, bound by rhythm, authorization, and contextual readiness.  
Propagation follows a disciplined cadence — no release without integrity, no signal without consequence.  
This protocol sustains coherence between the act of expression and the architecture of responsibility.  
**Linkage:** C4 (No Signal → No Release) ↔ E-Feedback — synchronizing release rhythm with feedback intelligence.

---

## G5 — Systemic Impact Loop  
The closure and reflection point of the entire execution chain.  
G5 collects post-action data — not merely performance indicators, but evidence of systemic shift.  
It evaluates whether the act expanded or diluted mission coherence, feeding that insight back into layers A through F.  
This loop transforms execution into a self-correcting organism, ensuring that growth does not equal drift.  
**Linkage:** A-Series (core mission) ↔ G3/G4 — continuous verification of mission integrity through action.

---

## G6 — Real-World Deployment / Public Interface (exec.aiflowlabs.studio)  
The materialization frontier where constitutional intent becomes public infrastructure.  
G6 defines the **Exec Domain** — a live operational environment translating internal intelligence into external presence.  
It includes:  
- **Exec Portal** — the actionable interface for curated releases, operations, and signals.  
- **Public Feedback Mechanism** — structured collection and semantic analysis of external responses.  
- **Deployment Integrity Gate** — a validation layer ensuring every public output reflects the mission’s ethical DNA.  

Here the system ceases to simulate execution and begins to *exist* in the world.  
The Exec Domain is not a publication site; it is the constitutional embodiment of agency — the visible proof that intelligence, integrity, and impact can coexist in one operational continuum.  
**Linkage:** G5 → External World — the junction of mission and manifestation.

---

### Closing Note  
The G-Series completes the vertical structure of the Constitution.  
From perception (A–C) to self-regulation (D–F) and finally to action (G), the system becomes whole only when impact and integrity are inseparable.  
Execution, therefore, is not the end of reasoning — it is its physical form.
